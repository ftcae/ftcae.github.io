Welcome to Fantastic Transport

 

The Fantastic Transport & Contracting & General Maintenance –Sole Proprietorship L.L.C was established in 2007 and is a general Sub contractor as well as an Earth works material supplier offering construction services in terms of Heavy vehicle transport, Site Analysis, Supply of backfilling materials, Excavation and removal of excavated material, Site Levelling and Clearing etc. We handle various filling materials such as, Gatch , Dune Sands , Black sand , White Sand, Red Dune sand , Gravels, Road Base, Sub Base & Aggregates etc..

Our Goal

 

The Transportation Division of Fantastic Transport & Contracting & General Maintenance Company is in the business of providing specialized "off-road" and "off-highway" state-of-the-art oilfield related equipment to the U.A.E Oil and Gas Industry.

We perform drill rig movement and relocation services which involve the hauling and rigging up of drilling rigs in challenging, desert terrain and extreme weather conditions.

Customer transportation logistics are pre-planned for each and every job through a network of terminals strategically located in the areas best known for oil and gas exploration activity.
