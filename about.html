Our Profile

 

The Fantastic Transport & Contracting & Gen. Maintenance –Sole Proprietorship L.L.C was established in 2007 and is a general Sub contractor as well as an Earth works material supplier offering construction services in terms of Heavy vehicle transport, Site Analysis, Supply of backfilling materials, Excavation and removal of excavated material, Site Levelling and Clearing etc. We handle various filling materials such as, Gatch , Dune Sands , Black sand , White Sand, Red Dune sand , Gravels, Road Base, Sub Base & Aggregates etc..

Fantastic Transport Company is a leader in providing value-added construction services to our customers by creating a successful partnership with them throughout the construction process. Our pledge is to establish lasting relationships with our customers by exceeding their expectations and gaining their trust through exceptional performance by every member of the construction team.

We have the vehicles and equipment for any type of land transportation . We have executed similar work for various projects , ADNOC projects in Abu Dhabi. We are capable to undertake any transportation project to our client’s satisfaction anywhere in the Gulf Region and the Middle East.

 

Our Mission

 

Dedication to providing quick and quality construction material transport, Equipment’s and management services to our customers. We will strive to implement a long term relationship with our clients, based on safety, quality, timely service and an anticipation of their needs. To help fulfill this mission, we will treat all employees fairly and involve them in the quality improvement process to insure responsiveness and cost effective work execution.
