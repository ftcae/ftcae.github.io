Services Overview

 

Our expertise lies in the specialized move, transfer and relocation of drilling rigs throughout the Middle East. We are responsible for managing the safe movement of materials and equipment as designated by our customers.

Our goal of zero defects starts with our people. We review each job separately and select our best people for every job. Pre-job planning and pre-job safety reports are prepared before, during and after job completion.

Our business units have excellent safety behaviors in place to achieve operational excellence. Safe work habits should be maintained at all times and busy seasons are not an excuse to act in a negligent manner.

Fantastic Transport Transportation Division will continue to develop an infrastructure that will support growth and accountability by including committed individuals who work together to meet the demand of productivity and safety.
